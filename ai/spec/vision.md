# Vision — Divulguei.Online

## Project Goal

Divulguei.Online is a **hyperlocal digital city guide and commerce platform** designed to digitize the economy and community life of small and medium Brazilian cities. It serves as the single digital hub where residents can discover businesses, buy and sell goods, find professionals, search for jobs, follow local events, and read community news — all in one place.

## Target Users

| User Type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Residents** | Citizens of small cities seeking local services, products, events, and news |
| **Business Owners** | Local shop and service owners who need digital visibility |
| **Freelancers & Professionals** | Tradespeople, consultants, and service providers looking for clients |
| **Job Seekers** | Residents searching for local employment opportunities |
| **Advertisers** | Individuals posting classified ads (buy/sell/rent) |
| **City Administrators** | Platform admins managing content, moderation, and city operations |

## Problem Solved

Small Brazilian cities lack a unified digital platform for local commerce and community information. Residents rely on fragmented WhatsApp groups, social media, and word-of-mouth. Businesses struggle with digital visibility. There is no organized, searchable directory of local services, classifieds, jobs, or events.

Divulguei.Online solves this by providing:

- A **structured, searchable** directory of everything local
- **AI-powered search** that understands natural language queries
- A **WhatsApp bot** that brings the platform to where users already are
- Tools for **anyone** to publish classifieds, services, and events without technical knowledge

## Value Proposition

1. **For Residents**: One place to find everything in their city — businesses, classifieds, jobs, events, news, emergency contacts
2. **For Businesses**: Affordable digital presence with visibility to the entire local community
3. **For the Platform**: Scalable city-by-city expansion model with premium business subscriptions as revenue
4. **For the Ecosystem**: Bridges the digital divide in underserved communities, enabling local economic growth

## Initial Market

- **Launch City**: Floresta, Pernambuco (PE), Brazil
- **Expansion Model**: City-by-city rollout, each city operates as an independent context with its own data, categories, and administration
